Falcon

Falcon is the fastest animal on the planet

  • Height: 9 to 19 inches.
  • Weight: 1.5 to 2.6 pounds.
  • Speed: 200 miles per hour.
  • Lifespan: around 15 years.

There are 37 species most species of falcon are dark brown or grey-colored with white, yellow and black spots and markings on the body.
